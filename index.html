Recovery outcomes are influenced by more than medical treatment alone. Internal stability and environmental pressures interact to either support or destabilize recovery.

The Holistic Stability Index™ evaluates these structural forces to help identify hidden pressures affecting recovery progress and case outcomes.

⏱ Estimated time: 3–5 minutes

Medical records are not required. Please answer based on the most accurate and current information available.

What This Evaluates
  • Physical wellness and injury-related functional capacity
  • Psychological stability and behavioral health impact
  • Psychosocial barriers and environmental pressures
  • Professional role stability and vocational outlook
  • Social drivers of health affecting recovery

Estimated completion time: 3–5 minutes

What You Will Receive
  • Holistic Stability Index™ score
  • Foundation Stability Model™ visualization
  • Primary vulnerability domain identification
  • Clinical Destabilizer Profile (Pain, Depression, Anxiety indicators)
